Flood Resources for Residents & Businesses


Another Brisbane City Council resource, this one contains information for both residents and businesses. It also includes information about financial programs for those impacted by floods.

Flood Resilient Homes Program


This program is being trialled in some areas of Brisbane. It involves a free in-home flood resilience assessment, recommendations to increase flood resilience and an incentive scheme to make the recommened renovations.

https://www.sustainablebrisbane.com.au/programs/floodwise/
